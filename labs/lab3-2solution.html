Icicle Creek

TAke a hike along iciclce creek and watch the majecstic stream flow. Enjoy the
soothing sounds of the rushing water while standing admist the surrounding
trees. This hike is rated a double boot because the climbing is moderatley
strenous. However, the land is immediately around the creek is flat and allows for
frequent rest stops.

View the Valley

This half day hike continues witha climb to one of the lower peaks (1,200 feet) of the Tierra Mountains. This
spot is chosen for its wonderful view of the valley below. Beautiful photos of the valley and the surrounding
woods can be taken at this spot.
